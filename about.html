Textiles


Nicholas Nybro works with different kinds of textiles in the making of his collections. The textiles are carefully selected so that they match the theme of his new SS18 The Body collection. The fascination of the human body and its different shapes, are what inspirers Nicholas Nybro in his creative work of designing unique clothing. The color of the textiles is ranging from delicate blue-ish and beige nuances to the darker warmer brown, so they represent all kinds of skin colors.
