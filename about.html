ABOUT

Nicholas Nybro is a danish fashion designer specializing in conceptual clothing, costumes and art direction.

The clothes are avaialble at Sabine Poupinel, Copenhagen K or from our online shop.


Textiles


Nicholas Nybro works with different kinds of textiles in the making of his collections. The textiles are carefully selected so that they match the theme of his new SS18 The Body collection. The fascination of the human body and its different shapes, are what inspirers Nicholas Nybro in his creative work of designing unique clothing. The color of the textiles is ranging from delicate blue-ish and beige nuances to the darker warmer brown, so they represent all kinds of skin colors.

Fashion Illustration


Apart of being a fashion designer Nicholas Nybro is also a Fashion Illustrator, who create beautiful illustrative work to showcase his concepts. Sketching is an important tool for Nicholas Nybro in his design process. Every time he gets inspired, he makes sure to sketch down fresh ideas, and therefor he will always have his sketchbook with him.
